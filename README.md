# 🏍️ Bikers Katte — Ride Together. Live Free.

> *A katte is the stone platform where a village gathers to talk. Ours just happens to have engines parked around it.*

---

## About Us

**Bikers Katte** is a tight-knit group of **16 riders** based out of **Bengaluru**. We ride together — short dawn runs, long highway hauls, ghat twisties, and everything in between. No egos, no pace pressure, no one left behind.

Led by **Captain Kiran** and **Vice Captain Shodan**, the katte is open to riders of any bike and any speed.

---

## Our Rides

We go on short and long trips together, share routes, tips, ride reports, and a lot of roadside chai recommendations along the way.

## The Katte Code

1. **Ride Your Own Ride** — No one is left behind. The slowest rider sets the pace.
2. **Gear Before Ego** — Full-face, gloves, boots. Every ride, every distance.
3. **The Road Is Shared** — We ride staggered, signal early, and respect every road we're on.
