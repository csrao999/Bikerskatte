Ride Your Own Ride

Gear Before Ego

Full-face, gloves, boots, spine. Every ride, every distance. There is no such thing as a short trip that deserves less protection.

Ride Safe

Non-negotiable. Ride captains check gear at flag-off, and yes, people have been sent home.

Helmet

Full-face, ISI or ECE certified. No half-shells on club rides.

Gloves & Boots

Knuckle protection and ankle cover. Hands go down first, always.

First Aid

Sweep rider carries a kit. Know where it is before you need it.

Formation

Staggered, two-second gap, lead and sweep marked in orange.

Signals

Hand signals briefed at flag-off. Repeat them down the line.

SOS

Every member profile carries blood group and an emergency contact, visible to ride captains offline. Club emergency line: 1800-000-0000.
